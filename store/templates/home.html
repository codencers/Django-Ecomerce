About Us

Welcome to EcoLink – Empowering Sustainability for Local Businesses At EcoLink, we believe that small actions lead to big change. Our mission is simple yet impactful: To connect small shopkeepers with affordable, eco-friendly packaging solutions through smart technology.

  • Connect shopkeepers with local, verified eco-packaging suppliers
  • Spread eco-awareness through gamified learning and rewards
  • Enable bulk orders, real-time tracking, and digital invoices

Join us on our journey to empower over 1 lakh+ Indian businesses by 2027, making sustainability simple, accessible, and impactful for everyone.

Why Choose EcoLink?

We’re committed to making sustainability simple and affordable for everyone — from local vendors to growing businesses across India. Here’s why customers choose us:

01 Why should small businesses trust EcoLink?

We specialize in affordable eco-friendly solutions designed for Indian shopkeepers, vendors, and entrepreneurs. Our products are high quality, reliable, and budget-friendly.

02 How does EcoLink help protect the environment?

By replacing plastic with biodegradable and reusable options, we help reduce daily waste and promote greener habits at the grassroots level—right where it matters most.

03 Is it easy to order and get support from EcoLink?

Yes! Our platform is simple to use, with fast delivery across Lucknow. Plus, our customer support team is friendly and ready to help you every step of the way.
